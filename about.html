History

Established in 2011, Wiki.ezvid.com is a free-to-use video wiki published by the California technology company Ezvid Inc. Wikis are generally architected around a video, whereby a user is introduced to a concept in the space of a four-to-seven minute video presentation, and then additional text, images, data, and links to third-party information sources are provided to add more detail to each topic. The gentle nature of Ezvid's introductions to thousands of topics renders the Wiki particularly useful for teaching environments. Ezvid Wiki evolved from the message board surrounding Ezvid For Windows, first released in 2010. In those years it has grown from a small user-generated forum into the largest and most comprehensive video wiki on the planet, providing useful, unbiased information and actionable guidance to hundreds of millions of users around the world, in thousands of knowledge categories.

In December, 2020, a product team at Google determined that Ezvid Wiki's service competed too directly with Google's in-house product advertising services, and manually demoted our pages in Google's search results, along with the pages of several similar websites. On one day, our incoming traffic, and traffic to many similar websites, went down by 85%. Updates to the Wiki are currently on hold, as we await the outcome of anti-trust litigation against Google in the United States on a federal and state level.
